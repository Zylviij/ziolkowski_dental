Welcome to Ziolkowski Dental!

Our office is located 10 minutes north of downtown Milwaukee in beautiful Shorewood, WI. We know you have many choices in your dental care. We also know how important your smile is. That's why we are proud to offer comfortable care in a relaxed atmosphere. We aim to provide high quality dentistry, great dental experiences, and results that surpass your expectations.

Here at Ziolkowski Dental we believe in a very conservative approach to dentistry. We will do the least invasive treatment needed to keep you healthy and comfortable. Often there are treatment options. We will thoroughly explain all of them to you and let you choose what is best for you. In whatever we do, our goal is to do it as well as possible. We do not want to be a usual or average office. We work hard to provide exceptional quality, comfort, and service, and are fortunate to have built a practice with patients who expect and receive a little extra!

Services what we can do for you

Cosmetic & Family Dentistry

We have the privilege of serving patients of all ages. In fact, we have many families where we see three (or even four) generations of patients: from toddlers to great-grandparents. Although we do many types of dental procedures, we really enjoy restoring or creating a beautiful smile, everything we do is considered to be cosmetic dentistry.

More Information

Nitrous Oxide

We have Nitrous Oxide, or laughing gas, to help ease the anxiety of dental appointments. However, for anxiety, we realize that there is nothing more important that painless, careful, and excellent work.

More Information

Headphones

For many patients, the noise of dental work is the worst part. Bring your headphones or use ours and relax with your favorite music.

Internet

Ask us for the password and you can access the Internet on a your devices while you are here.

Emergencies

We allow time in our schedule every day to accommodate for emergencies. If you are a regular patient at our office and you are in pain, we will do what is necessary to make you comfortable.

Cosmetic Imaging

We use special dental imaging software to create "before and after" photographs of your smile. We can design a new smile and get a visual representation of how it will look when we are finished.

Sports Dentistry

As the team dental office for the Milwaukee Admirals professional hockey team since 1985, we have seen nearly every injury you could imagine. We have extensive experience in getting injured athletes (and unfortunate non-athletes) out of pain and looking good quickly.

More Information

Denture & Partials

If tooth loss is unavoidable, removable replacement partial dentures are an option if some natural teeth remain. Full dentures are needed if all teeth are missing and implants are not possible.

More Information

Smile Makeovers

Today, anyone can have a beautiful, healthy-looking smile. Broken, injured, crowded, missing, or other unattractive teeth can be imporoved with dental procedures like: whitening, veneers, crowns, bridges, cosmetic bonding, and implants. There are often several ways that we can achieve the result that we desire, and can discuss each of them.

Implants

Implants are used to permanently replace a tooth or several teeth. They can also be used to help support a loose denture or partial denture. The process of placing and restoring an implant is usually a team approach and involves the assistance of an oral surgeon or periodentist (gum specialist).

More Information

Teeth Whitening

Teeth that are discolored from staining, age, medications, or genetics can be whitened. How white you want them to be is your choice. We can discuss this with you.

Veneers

Dental veneers are thin layers of porcelain or ceramic that are placed over the front of a tooth. Often used as a cosmetic restoration, they can beautifully convert worn, crooked, chipped, uneven, or discolored teeth.

Fillings

When a tooth has small holes (cavities), it can often be repaired by cleaning out and rebuilding the tooth with a tooth-colored composite resin or silver filling. Due to the cost, gold is seldom used today. We can decide which is best for you.

Crowns

Sometimes a tooth is so badly damaged or decayed that a filling will not be strong enough to last very long. In this case we may recommend a crown, which is fabricated in a lab, covers the whole tooth, and is many times stronger than a filling.

More Information

Bridges

A bridge is a permanently cemented replacement for a missing tooth or teeth. It is basically three or more crowns which are attached together and placed on permanent teeth to replace the missing teeth or tooth.

More Information

Gum Therapy

Healthy gums are vital in maintaining the health of the teeth, mouth, and body. Our hygienists are highly skilled at thoroughly and comfortably removing the plaque and tartar that cause gum disease. Preventing disease is a priority for us and we will help you set up and follow a program to avoid problems in the future.

More Information

Root Canals

Sometimes the nerve endings of a tooth become infected or die. This can result in pressure or pain inside of the tooth. Often root canal therapy can relieve the pressure and eliminate the pain.

More Information

Cleanings

Your appointment with one of our hygienists is so much more than "just" a cleaning. Routine care will help prevent and identify any oral health problems you may have. At your visit we:

  • Review medical history: certain conditions and medications can have an impact on oral health
  • Perform an oral cancer screening
  • Take necessary x-rays
  • Check your gums for signs of Gingivitis or Periodontal Disease; to do this we measure the pocket (space around the tooth)
  • Remove plaque, tartar, and harmful bacteria
  • Provide ways to improve and maintain good oral health
More Information

Patient Resources

As a member of the Academy of General Dentistry, I recommend their website KnowYourTeeth.com for all of your online dental advice. They have loads of information and resources for families who want to know more about excellent dental care and oral health. Whether you are an expectant mom, a teen dealing with braces, a grandparent, or someone in between, they have the answers for you. What's more, kids will find games, quizzes and other ways to make learning about dental care more fun!

Financial Information

Philosophy

Our fees are based on the skill, time and cost involved in providing services and is not always the same as those reimbursed by insurance companies. For those patients with insurance, we will be happy to complete and submit all forms pertaining to your claim. However, insurance companies, plans and benefits vary greatly, and not all charges may be covered or completely paid by insurance.

To avoid misunderstandings, we encourage discussion of services and fees with the front office staff prior to treatment. Unless specific payment arrangements have been approved by this office, fees for professional services are due and payable the day services are rendered. Payment can be made by cash, check, debit card, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, or Discover.

Through this policy, it is our intent to keep dental costs down while maintaining a high level of professional care.
